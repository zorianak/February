Slide Down || Slide Up Website Designs

This section includes the designs made while working as a freelance website designer and sites for Business Development Institute.

The Cyber Smoothie Cafe
As both a smoothie shop and an Internet cafe, the Cyber Smoothie Cafe needed a theme that relayed its tropical origins, showed its products, as well as enticed visitors to come to this cute shop. As a client of the Cyber Smoothie Cafe, I saw its charms, and gave the site not only custom vector images alongside the photos approved by the owner, but also a color scheme which really stirs the taste buds!

DesignJournal Community
LiveJournal is one of the most popular free blogging platforms in the entire world, and so creating a CSS template for the Official Design Journal was a real treat - and one done with pride.

Roy Audio
This site features a DIY audio theme - because it was created for a client whose life work centers around creating high fidelity speakers. It may seem graphics-heavy, but really, it was designed to load quickly, meet standards, and as with all design jobs go - look good in all browsers.

Charles Rich Accounting
This design for Charles Rich Accounting features a logo design, as well as a light, flexible, business-type design. Flexible, it works very well in other browsers, and was designed to load quickly and officially while at the same time having a very crisp, business-like look.

CIR Realty with Jeanette Jackson
When it comes to having a simple realty site, this one nailed it for the customer's needs. Being very minimalistic and having the mindset of what the client required, this design was made for quick loading and to be especially informative, while at the same time, remain to be aesthetically pleasing.

Superior Copper Piping
The last thing you want to think about is your home or office's piping, and thus, get copper repiping! This site was done to give a more artistic feel, while at the same time matching the client's desires for a one-page site that does everything they need it to do - explain why their services were important, provide contact information, and look appealing. All three were met with great satisfaction.

jQuery Programming for Spriggs
The jQuery programming for this site's programming page was a pleasure to do, including a pop-out informational box for the products, as well as a sliding accordion menu for each type of product. The client absolutely loved it!

Lynns Lockshop
The client wanted one simple, one-page site for their clients to be able to find and contact them. This site delivers just that, with a quick load time and a clean, elegant look and feel.

Slide Down || Slide Up Print Designs

This section includes the designs made for print work including posters and theatre programs, as well as party menus.

My Jeneration Menu
This menu design was created utilizing Photoshop and InDesign. The client provided the colors and general style that they wanted for the menu, and we took it and matched their theme of exotic elegance - much to their pleasant surprise!

One Act Show Poster
For this design, the cliented wanted a poster that conveyed that the students at their theatre had taken over the theatre, and to give it a crime scene investigation look. Designed in Photoshop and InDesign, and later converted to Microsoft Pagemaker in order to create the program.

The Taming of the Shrew Show Poster
This poster design features a hand-drawn Petruchio carrying a rather perturbed Katerina off, just as the director had envisioned. It was created with graphite pencil sketches, Photoshop, and InDesign, and later converted for Microsoft Pagemaker in order to create the program for the show.
